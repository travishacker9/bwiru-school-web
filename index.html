About Our School

Our Legacy

Founded in 1920, Bwiru Boys School has grown from humble beginnings to become a regional leader in olevel and Alevel' education, with a current enrollment of 1,200 students and 80 teaching staff.

Our History

From 2 classrooms to a 50-acre campus with modern facilities

Mission & Vision

Developing holistic individuals through academic and co-curricular excellence

School History

Timeline of our school's development and achievements:

1975

School founded with 2 classrooms and 50 students under founding principal Mr. THOMAS WEREMA

1985

First CSEE class graduated with 80% university qualification. School expanded to 10 classrooms.

2005

Science lab constructed, enabling students to take practical science exams on-site for the first time

2010

Our school get nobel prize in tanzania for being best school

2015

Celebrated 30th anniversary. School ranked in top 50 nationally for academic performance.

2024

New computer lab opened with 50 workstations. School website launched.

2025

Our school received first PMC'S class

2025

Our school received first WEB builders mathias kengele and alliance

2025

Current enrollment reaches 1,200 students. Ranked #1 in county for 5th consecutive year.
